Mobile Banking

Mobile banking is the act of making financial transactions on a mobile device (cell phone, tablet, etc.). This activity can be as simple as a bank sending fraud or usage activity to a client's cell phone or as complex as a client paying bills or sending money abroad.


Online banking refers to any transaction that is conducted over the internet, usually through your banking provider's website. ... A mobile banking app usually allows you to access similar, (although sometimes slightly fewer), features that you would find when online banking, but it must be done on a smartphone or tablet.


Mobile banking allows you to perform many of the same activities as online banking using a smartphone or tablet instead of a desktop computer. However, simply accessing the bank's website on a mobile device is not the only method of mobile banking. ... Logging into a bank's mobile website. Using a mobile banking app.
